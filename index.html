Privacy Policy for Odoji

Last updated: January 25, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Odoji. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Character profiles and settings you create
  • Chat messages and conversations
  • User profile information
  • World descriptions and settings
  • API keys for third-party services (stored locally)

Automatically Collected Information

  • App usage analytics (if enabled)
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Device information (iOS version, device model)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the Odoji app functionality
  • Generate AI responses through the Replicate API
  • Store your characters, chats, and settings locally on your device
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs

Data Storage and Security

  • All your data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not store your personal data on our servers
  • API keys are stored securely in your device's keychain
  • Chat data sent to Replicate API is processed according to their privacy policy

Third-Party Services

Odoji uses the following third-party services:

  • Replicate API: For AI model processing. Please review Replicate's Privacy Policy
  • DeepSeek V3 Model: AI model used for generating responses

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored in the app
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Export your data through app settings
  • Control which features have access to your data

Children's Privacy

Odoji is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@odoji.app
  • Support Page: odoji.app/support
